PMP Certification Progress Tracker

Go to the Tracker

An interactive, web-based progress tracker to help you prepare for the Project Management Professional (PMP)® certification exam. This tool allows you to check off tasks as you master them and visually track your overall progress.

🚀 Live Demo

You can access the live version of this tracker here: https://lancelotet.github.io/pmp-track/

✨ Features

  • Comprehensive Task List: Includes a detailed list of tasks organized by the three PMP domains: People, Process, and Business Environment.
  • Interactive Checkboxes: Easily mark tasks as complete.
  • Progress Bar: A visual representation of your overall progress.
  • Percentage Counter: See your exact completion percentage.
  • Local Storage: Your progress is automatically saved in your browser, so you can close the page and return later without losing your data.
  • Reset Functionality: A "Reset" button to clear all your progress and start over.

🛠️ How to Use

  1. Open the Tracker: Access the live link provided in the repository's "About" section.
  2. Check Off Tasks: As you study and feel confident in a topic, check the box next to it.
  3. Monitor Your Progress: The progress bar and percentage will update automatically.
  4. Continue Later: Your progress is saved, so you can come back anytime.
  5. Reset if Needed: If you want to start fresh, click the "Reset Progress" button.

🔧 Built With

  • HTML5: For the structure and content of the web page.
  • CSS3: For styling and making the tracker visually appealing.
  • JavaScript (ES6): For interactivity, progress calculation, and saving data to local storage.
